## Registration

Q: How do I register?
A:
1. Open Registration/Login.
2. Choose Register.
3. Enter required information.
4. Verify using SMS verification code.
5. Complete registration.

---------------------------------------

Q: Why am I not receiving my verification code?

A:
- Check your phone supports SMS.

- Verify the phone number.
- Check your email spam folder.
- Verification codes expire after 5 minutes.

---------------------------------------

Q: Can I change my username?

A:
No.
Usernames cannot be changed once created.

---------------------------------------

Q: How do I change my password?

A:
1. Login
2. Account Management
3. Change Password
4. Enter current password
5. Enter new password
6. Save

---------------------------------------

Q: Password requirements

A:
- 14-20 characters
- One uppercase
- One lowercase
- One number
- One special character

---------------------------------------

Q: Can I change my email?

A:
Yes.
Navigate to:
Account Management
→ Change Email

---------------------------------------

Q: Can I change my phone number?

A:
Yes.
Navigate to:
Account Management
→ Change Phone Number

---------------------------------------

Q: Verification code expired

A:
Verification codes are valid for 5 minutes.

---------------------------------------

Q: Session timeout

A:
Users are automatically logged out after
15 minutes of inactivity.

---------------------------------------

Q: Can I delete my account?

A:
Users cannot delete accounts themselves.
Support must process account deletion.

---------------------------------------

Q: Can I register using an old username?

A:
No.
Each username must be unique.

---------------------------------------

Q: Contact Support

A:
If the chatbot cannot resolve your issue,
contact the LBL Service Center.
